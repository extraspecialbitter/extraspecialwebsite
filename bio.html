Want my Biograph?



Paul David Mena made his debut in Life as we know it an unspecified number of years ago in the quasi-conscious state of New York. Through some obvious oversight, he graduated from Hofstra University in 1982 with a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science. Paul currently draws a salary from Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution where he ostensibly functions as a DevOps Engineer. Paul is happily married to Mary and has five children and a cantankerous dingo. In his spare time, Paul writes poetry, particularly haiku. Another ongoing project is an unauthorized autobiography, which will be written posthumously.
